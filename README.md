# Accounting Skill

International accounting & bookkeeping skill for AI coding agents. Covers IFRS, US GAAP, global tax systems, e-invoicing compliance, entity types, multi-currency accounting, and regional software recommendations for 169+ countries.

## Install

```bash
npx skills add Xzeroone/accounting-skill@accounting
```

## What It Covers

- **Accounting Standards** — IFRS, US GAAP, IFRS for SMEs, and all major regional variants (UK GAAP, German HGB, French PCG, Japanese J-GAAP, Indian Ind AS, Chinese CAS, Swiss GAAP FER, etc.)
- **Tax Systems** — Corporate tax rates, VAT/GST/Sales Tax by country, advance payment schedules, deductions, freelancer obligations, Pillar Two Global Minimum Tax
- **Entity Types** — LLC, Ltd, GmbH, SARL, Pty Ltd, Pte Ltd, KK, LTDA, AG, and more by country
- **E-Invoicing** — Clearance vs post-audit models, 90+ country mandates (NF-e, CFDI, SDI, KSeF, ZATCA, RO e-Factura, SEE, etc.)
- **Accounting Software** — Regional recommendations for Europe, Asia-Pacific, Latin America, Middle East & Africa
- **Multi-Currency** — FX transaction handling, functional/presentation currency, country-specific rules
- **Bookkeeping Routines** — Weekly, monthly, quarterly, and annual checklists
- **Revenue Recognition** — IFRS 15 / ASC 606, cash vs accrual, deferred revenue
- **Financial Reports** — P&L, cash flow, balance sheet interpretation
- **Payroll & Employer Obligations** — Global payroll requirements, employer contribution rates by country
- **Transfer Pricing & Double Taxation** — OECD guidelines, arm's length principle, DTAs
- **Tax Credits & Incentives** — R&D tax credits, regional incentives, startup tax breaks
- **Terminology** — US vs UK/International accounting terms

## Data Freshness

Tax rates and e-invoicing mandates change frequently. Data compiled in **2026-Q1**. Always verify with local authorities.
